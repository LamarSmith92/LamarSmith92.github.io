About

Hello, I am Lamar Smith, a Bay Area-Based Junior Web Developer. I am 24 years old and, I was born in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. I grew up playing sports until I graduated high school. After high school I decided to get into personal fitness and speed training. I coached kids mostly in urban areas to keep them out of trouble and be a positive mentor. After moving to Colorado with my with my older brother last year, who was teaching himself how to code, I decided to dive into it and give it a shot. It turned out to be something I really enjoyed doing. I am currently taking a web development immersive course at General Assembly in San francisco, California and, someday plan to work for a Fortune 500 tech company or local start-up. I am very motivated and prepared to challenging myself to accomplish set goals. My goal overall is to give back to my community by teaching minorities in low-income areas how to code in a cool, fun way, and that anything is possible.
